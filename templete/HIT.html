1. Task Contents

Participants will imagine a bar graph in front of them, listen to an audio explanation of the bar graph, and then estimate the detailed values of each bar.

2. Ethical Considerations

We will take the following ethical considerations in cooperation with the research:

  1. Cooperation in this research is voluntary and not compulsory.
  2. Even if participants refuse to cooperate in this research, participants will not suffer any disadvantage. Participants can quit the cooporation at any time but, in this case, rewards will not be paid.
  3. The data from this research will be stored on computers and servers accessible only to the researchers participating in this research.
  4. The survey results will be published in academic papers, etc., but participants will not be identified.
  5. After the research is completed, all data that could identify individuals will be destroyed.
